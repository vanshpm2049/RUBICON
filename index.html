No Segregation Infrastructure

Mixed waste renders the organic fraction unusable for biogas or composting — the feedstock is wasted before it reaches a plant.

Fossil Fuel Lock-In

Subsidised LPG creates structural dependency. Commercial users pay the full import-linked price — exposed to global commodity swings.

Missing Middle Market

Large plants need municipalities. Small plants lack commercial viability. The institutional segment is entirely unserved.

Methane Emissions Crisis

Landfill waste emits methane — 28× more potent than CO₂ as a greenhouse gas. Every ton gasified is a direct climate win.

Technology Moat

AI/ML Makes Our Plants
Smarter Than Every Competitor

01 · FEEDSTOCK

Feedstock Optimisation
